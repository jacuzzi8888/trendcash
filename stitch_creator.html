# Top 10 Tech Hubs in Nigeria: 2024 Guide

The Nigerian technological landscape has experienced an unprecedented surge in digital innovation over the last decade. As the "Silicon Valley of Africa," the nation continues to cultivate ecosystems that attract global venture capital and nurture homegrown unicorns.

## 1. Lagos - The Silicon Valley of Africa

Lagos remains the undisputed heavyweight champion of the Nigerian tech scene. With high-density hubs like Yaba—colloquially known as "Yabacon Valley"—the city serves as the nerve center for startups like Flutterwave, Paystack, and Andela. The availability of diverse talent pools and proximity to financial institutions makes it the primary destination for founders.
